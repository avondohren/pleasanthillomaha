Links

FindAGrave.com

Find A Grave's mission is to find, record, and present final disposition information from around the world as a virtual cemetery experience. This web site enables searches for persons buried in cemeteries around the world. All entries in Find A Grave have been entered by volunteers. While the majority of the graves at Pleasant Hill have been catalogued on Find A Grave, many have not. Pleasant Hill Cemetery has not compiled this information, nor does it vouch for its accuracy. We are simply providing a link to another web site that we think might be useful for our members.
